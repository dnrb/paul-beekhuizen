Musette
  • After an anonymous French instruments of the 18th century.
  • Bellows blown
  • Cylindrical bore
  • Grand chalumeau: (f'/f#')g' - g" (chromatic scale, with the help of 6 keys)
  • Petit chalumeau: (6 keys) amol" - d'"
  • Drones: shuttle drones: G - g - d' - g' and c - g - c' - g'
  • Made of ebony; silver keys - a'= 415 Hz
  • This is the standard-musette; 95% of the music was written for this instrument.
  • The musette can also be made in the old pitch of a' = 392 Hz. It is then possible to play with modern tuned instruments. The musette plays in G; the other instruments transpose to F.
  • Price: fl. 7250/$ 3300
  • In preparation an instrument based on the musette in the Pitt Rivers Museum in Oxford. This instrument has wider bores and sounds louder.
